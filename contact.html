Got Questions?

What do I have to bring for studio classes?

For studio classes, we will provide all the materials needed. The only things that we encourage our clients to bring are comfortable clothes and a bottle of water. 

How can I cancel a subscription?

Closing your subscription is easy and hassle-free. Simply log in to your account on our website, navigate to the subscription settings, and you'll find a straightforward option to cancel. We believe in flexibility, so there are no additional costs or hidden fees – you can cancel anytime, aligning your wellness journey with your own rhythm.

How can I use a giftcard?

To use your gift card at our yoga studio, simply present it during checkout for any service of your choice. Enjoy the flexibility, as there's no time limit—redeem it at your own pace for a rejuvenating yoga experience!

How can I take part in a trip?

To embark on a yoga trip abroad with us, simply visit our website or contact our studio. Explore our upcoming retreats, choose your destination, and follow the easy registration process. Secure your spot, pack your bags, and get ready for a transformative yoga experience in a picturesque setting. Namaste and see you on the mat!
