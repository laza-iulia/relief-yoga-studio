Relief Yoga Studio
throughout the years

2003

The creation of the studio

Founded by a passionate yogi named Sarah, the studio started with just a handful of students eager to explore the practice of yoga. In the early days, the studio started humbly, with a few dedicated instructors offering classes in a modest space. The community grew steadily as word spread about our studio, one where everyone, regardless of age, appearance, sexuality, fitness level or experience, could find solace and strength on their yoga mats.

2010

New Location

As the studio expanded, we moved to a larger space to accommodate the growing community. This new location allowed additional workshops and specialized courses. Sarah's dream of inclusivity became even more tangible as the studio welcomed individuals with diverse backgrounds and abilities.

2015

YogaEverywhere Trips

We took a bold step forward by embracing a new opportunity: that of taking part in yoga trips within the YogaEverywhere program. And since then, our studio has evolved so much that we have been able to even organize our own trips!

2018

Yogi Whiskerpaws

As Sarah arrives to the studio doors one rainy morning, she notices a tiny cat that
found refuge under our stairs. After days of gentle coaxing and leaving out food, the once wary cat became a cherished studio companion. Since then, Yogi Whiskerpaws' presence has become a symbol of the unexpected joys that unfold when openness and compassion guide your journey.

2020

Online Classes

Faced with unprecedented challenges, our community stood strong. The global pandemic forced us to adapt and innovate. Live-streamed classes, virtual workshops and an enhanced online community became prioritary. Through these changes, we discovered new ways to connect, support one another, and foster a sense of unity in a time of physical distance.

2023

Present Day

Our yoga studio stands as a testament to the power of resilience and community. The journey from a small, local studio to a globally connected community has been marked by unwavering dedication to our mission. As we look ahead, we remain committed to making yoga accessible to all, ensuring that our welcoming and inclusive space continues to be a source of inspiration, healing, and transformation for many years to come.

Breathe, Believe, Achieve: Our dedicated team, weaving wellness into every yoga moment

Sarah, CEO

"I wanted to pass on the joy and peace that yoga brought to me throughout the years"

As a CEO, Sarah encourages a collaborative and supportive environment within the center. She believes in the transformative power of yoga and is driven by the mission to make it accessible to everyone. Sarah's leadership style is characterized by empathy, open communication and a genuine concern for the personal growth of both her staff and clients.

Leah, Instructor for Beginners

"Anyone can master this practice and become more at peace with themselves"

Leah's classes are a blend of foundational poses, mindful breathing exercises, and a touch of humor to lighten the mood, taking the time to explain each posture properly. Leah's emphasis on proper alignment and the importance of listening to one's body fosters a safe and nurturing environment for those taking their first steps into yoga.

Jason, Yoga Master

"Yoga gives you the continuous opportunity to learn something new"

Despite his profound expertise, Jason is a humble and approachable teacher who firmly believes that the path of learning in yoga is infinite. His classes are a blend of challenging poses, mindful breathwork, and philosophical insights. Jason encourages his students to embrace the process, acknowledging that each day on the mat is an opportunity for growth and discovery.

Hannah, Advanced Yoga Trainer

"Yoga was my anchor during challenging times"

In her sessions, Hannah creates an atmosphere of encouragement. Her classes go beyond the physical postures, weaving in elements of self reflection and personal empowerment. Hannah is known for tailoring her teachings to each student's unique needs, recognizing that everyone's journey in yoga is transformative in its own way.

Yogi Whiskerpaws, cat

"Meow meow meow meow meow meow"

This isn't your typical yoga instructor; Yogi Whiskerpaws brings a whole new meaning to the art of cat poses and mindful stretches. Watch in awe as Yogi Whiskerpaws effortlessly demonstrates the true essence of cat-asanas. From the graceful "Upward Feline" to the meditative "Paw-sana," each movement is a lesson in fluidity and grace. Prepare to be inspired by the master of the mat!
